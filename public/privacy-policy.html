1. What this app does

FinCalci is a free, offline-capable calculator app with 20 calculator tiles and 60+ tools designed for Indian users. This privacy policy explains exactly what data the app collects, what it does not collect, and how your information is handled.

2. Data we collect

FinCalci uses Firebase Analytics by Google. This means the app does collect some data automatically when you use it. Here is exactly what is collected and what is not:

Data typeCollected?Details
Name, email, phone numberNoNever collected. No login or registration exists.
Your calculation inputs (loan amount, salary, tax details)NoAll calculations happen on your device. We cannot see your numbers.
Khata Book customer names, phone numbers, transactionsNoStored only on your device in localStorage. Never transmitted.
Expense entries, budget dataNoStored only on your device. Never transmitted.
Photos, contacts, files, location (GPS)NoApp does not request any device permissions.
Which calculators you openYesFirebase logs calculator names (e.g., "EMI opened"), not your inputs.
Session durationYesHow long you use the app per visit.
Feature usage (share, PDF export, theme change)YesTells us which features are popular.
Device info (model, OS, screen size)Yes (automatic)Collected automatically by Firebase SDK. We use this to ensure the app works on all devices.
Approximate location (country/city)Yes (automatic)Derived from IP address by Firebase. No GPS access. Used only for aggregate geographic analytics.
Anonymous device identifierYes (automatic)Firebase assigns a random ID to your app instance. This is not tied to your name, email, or Google account.

In plain language: We can see "someone in Mumbai opened the EMI calculator for 45 seconds on a Samsung phone." We cannot see "Rahul calculated a Rs 50 lakh loan at 8.5% for 20 years."

3. Why we collect analytics

  • To understand which calculators are popular and which need improvement
  • To fix bugs and crashes on specific devices
  • To make decisions about what to build next

We do not use analytics data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

4. Data stored on your device

The following data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device:

  • Calculator history and saved results
  • Khata Book customers, transactions, and balances
  • Expense entries, budgets, and categories
  • Split group sessions and member data
  • Theme preference, favorites, streaks, and settings
  • Last-used input values for calculators

You can delete all local data at any time by clearing your browser data or using the "Reset" option in Settings. You can also use Backup/Restore to export or import your data.

5. Third-party services

  • Firebase Analytics (Google) — usage analytics as described above. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Free exchange rate APIs — the app fetches currency, gold, silver, and mutual fund rates from public APIs. These requests contain your IP address (as all internet requests do) but no personal information.

We do not use Google Ads, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising or tracking SDKs beyond Firebase Analytics.

6. Data sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any data with third parties. Firebase Analytics data is processed by Google under their data processing terms. We do not export or transfer this data to any other service.

7. Children's privacy

FinCalci is a general-purpose calculator suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The analytics data collected (calculator usage, device info) does not identify individual users, including children. If you believe a child's personal data has been collected, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Data security

We take security seriously:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • No user accounts or passwords exist to be breached
  • All user inputs are sanitized to prevent injection attacks
  • Content Security Policy headers restrict what code can run
  • Local data is accessible only through your browser — we have no server-side access to it

9. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all your data is already on your device in localStorage
  • Delete your data — clear browser data or use Settings → Reset
  • Opt out of analytics — disable JavaScript in your browser (this will also disable the app)
  • Request information — email us at fincalci.help@gmail.com

10. Advertisements

FinCalci contains no advertisements. We do not serve ads from any ad network.

11. Disclaimer & limitation of liability

FinCalci is provided "as is" for informational and educational purposes only. The calculations, rates, and data provided by this app are estimates and should not be relied upon for financial, tax, legal, or investment decisions.

  • Gold & silver rates are derived from international spot prices and may not reflect actual Indian retail rates (which include import duty and local premiums). Always verify with your jeweller.
  • Currency exchange rates are indicative mid-market rates. Actual bank/forex rates will differ.
  • Tax calculations are estimates. Consult a Chartered Accountant for exact tax filing.
  • EMI, SIP, FD, PPF calculations are mathematical estimates. Actual amounts vary by bank/institution.
  • Calorie and BMI data is approximate and not a substitute for medical advice.

FinCalci Apps, its developer(s), and contributors shall not be held liable for any loss, damage, or consequence arising from the use of this app or reliance on its calculations.

12. Google Play Data Safety

For the Google Play Data Safety section, FinCalci discloses:

  • Data collected: App activity (calculator usage, feature usage), Device or other IDs (anonymous Firebase instance ID), Diagnostics (crash logs if any)
  • Data shared: None
  • Data encrypted in transit: Yes (HTTPS)
  • Data deletion: Users can clear local data via browser settings. Firebase analytics data is retained per Google's retention policy (default 14 months) and cannot be individually deleted as it is anonymous and aggregated.
